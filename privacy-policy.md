PRIVACY POLICY
Suraksha — Personal Safety App
Last updated: June 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Suraksha ("the App") is a personal safety application designed for 
women. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, 
how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Suraksha, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information you provide
- Your name (for personalisation only)
- Phone numbers of your trusted contacts
- Community reports you submit (stored anonymously)

2.2 Information collected automatically
- Your precise GPS location (only when app is open or SOS is active)
- Device type and operating system (for crash reporting only)

2.3 Information we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your email unless you voluntarily link it
- We do not collect payment information
- We do not collect social media profiles
- We do not track you when the app is closed

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information only for:
- Sending SOS alerts with your location to your trusted contacts
- Sharing your live location during active safety sessions
- Showing community-reported unsafe areas near you
- Improving app stability via anonymous crash reports

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to anyone
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for profiling or marketing
- Share your location with anyone except your chosen trusted contacts

4. LOCATION DATA

Suraksha uses your precise location for:
- SOS alerts (sends your coordinates to trusted contacts)
- Live location sharing sessions (only when you start one)
- Showing unsafe areas near you on the community map

Location is never sent to our servers in the background.
Location sharing stops the moment you end a session or cancel SOS.

5. TRUSTED CONTACTS

Phone numbers you add as trusted contacts are:
- Stored only on your device and in your private account
- Used only to send SOS alerts via SMS or WhatsApp
- Never shared with other users or third parties
- Deleted immediately when you remove a contact

6. COMMUNITY REPORTS

When you submit an unsafe area or route report:
- Your name and phone number are never attached to the report
- Only the location, incident type, and time of day are stored
- Reports are reviewed before appearing publicly
- You cannot be identified from your report

7. DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud 
database provider with encryption at rest and in transit.
Supabase's privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Crash reports are processed by Sentry.
Sentry's privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

8. DATA RETENTION

- Your profile and contacts: kept until you delete your account
- SOS event history: kept for 12 months then auto-deleted
- Community reports: kept for 24 months
- Crash logs: kept for 90 days

9. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access all data we hold about you
- Correct any inaccurate information
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

Suraksha is intended for users aged 18 and above.
We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.
If you believe a minor has used the app, contact us immediately.
