Objective

Redesign the entire Suraksha mobile application UI into a modern, premium, Google Material 3 + Apple Human Interface inspired safety application.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT modify business logic, APIs, Supabase integration, Firebase integration, RevenueCat integration, authentication, database schema, navigation routes, or existing functionality.

Only redesign the UI/UX.

⸻

Design Philosophy

The application should feel like:

* Google Pixel
* Uber
* Apple Find My
* Google Maps
* Life360

The interface must feel:

* Modern
* Premium
* Calm
* Minimal
* Trustworthy
* Safety focused

Avoid clutter.

Everything should be accessible using one hand.
